About

We believe that people can enjoy snooker and reach their full potential through engaging in tournamnets and by playing in the club whilst having fun doing so.

Our Values

Inclusion

We work to make a positive and inclusive club where the talents and contribution of each member can be utilised. Our idea is to train in new members whilst keeping a element of fun invovled to ensure everyone enjoys thier time with the club.

Respect

We show consideration for and value one another. We acknowledge and respect the unique personalities of each member, thier ideas, play styles, backgrounds, experiences and talents. We strive to have our club respectful to all.

Integrity

We train cooperatively and engage openly and honestly with all our members.

Courage

We bring courage to our club and we are willing to take on new methods and way to play going forward. We lead boldly and act decisively for good.
